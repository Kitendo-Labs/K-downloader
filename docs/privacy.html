Kitendo Stream Downloader

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Kitendo Stream Downloader ("the extension") is a browser extension that detects HLS (.m3u8) and DASH (.mpd) video streams on the page you are viewing and lets you download them to your own device. This policy explains exactly what the extension does and does not do with your data.

The short version

The extension collects no personal data and sends nothing to us or to any third party. It has no servers, no analytics, no telemetry, and no tracking. Everything it does happens locally inside your own browser.

What the extension accesses

  • Network requests on the active tab - The extension watches network requests to detect stream manifest URLs (.m3u8 / .mpd). Detection happens locally in your browser. These URLs are never transmitted anywhere.
  • The current tab's address and title - Used only to associate detected streams with the tab you are on and to suggest a sensible download filename.
  • Stream content you choose to download - When you click download, the extension fetches the video segments directly from the stream's own origin server (the same server your browser already loads the video from) and assembles them into a single file saved through your browser's normal download mechanism. The video data flows only between that origin server and your device.

What the extension stores

  • Session storage only - Detected stream URLs (per tab) and your chosen "parallel downloads" preference are kept in chrome.storage.session. This data lives only for the current browser session and is automatically cleared when you close your browser. It never leaves your device.

What the extension does NOT do

  • It does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.
  • It does not use analytics, advertising, fingerprinting, or tracking of any kind.
  • It does not send any data to the developer or to any third-party server.
  • It does not sell or share data, because it does not collect any.
  • It does not load or execute any remote code; all logic ships inside the extension.

Permissions and why they are needed

  • webRequest - to observe network requests and detect stream manifests.
  • downloads - to save the assembled video file to your device.
  • storage - to remember detected streams and your preference for the session.
  • activeTab / host access - so detection can work on whatever page you are viewing, since a stream can appear on any website.
  • offscreen - to assemble large video files in a context with sufficient memory.

Responsible use

The extension is intended for downloading video content that you are authorized to download - for example, your own content or media you have permission to save. You are responsible for complying with the terms of service of the websites you use and with applicable copyright law.
